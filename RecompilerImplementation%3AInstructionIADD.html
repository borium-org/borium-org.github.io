Java Recompiler: Instruction Implementation

IADD instruction operation:

  1. Pop right entry from the operand stack
  2. Pop left entry from the operand stack.
  3. Verify that both entry types are 'int'.
  4. Surround left and right expressions with parentheses and push the sum to the operand stack. Parentheses are necessary to maintain operator precedence that is lost in stack-based postfix implementation.
